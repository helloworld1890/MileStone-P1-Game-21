The Game of 21

1. 3 tries on Roll the dice and try not to go over 21

2. Hold the Score you want to keep, but holding over 21 will reset your Score

3. End turn is if you don't want to keep the rolling value. This will also end your turn

4. New Game is if you want to start all over again
